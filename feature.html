Total Station

Total Station is the advanced electronic / computerised machine for topographic surveying. It is integrated with an electronic distance meter (EDM) to read slope distances from the instrument to a particular point.The distances, angles, and co-ordinates are measured by infrared signals, with great accuracy.It can store data of distance, angles, levels which can be downloaded to a computer later. The computer will use this data to generate a map and get prints from the printer. Total station is used widely in all civil works all over the world as it saves time and maintains accuracy.
